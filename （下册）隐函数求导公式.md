# About Single Formula
## 隐函数存在定理1:
 设函数$ F(x, y)$在点$ P(x_0, y_0)$ 的某个邻域内具有连续的偏导数，且$ F(x_0, y_0)  = 0$且，$ F_y(x_0, y_0) \neq 0$那么方程$ F(x, y) = 0$ 在点$ P(x_0, y_0)$ 的邻域内恒能唯一确定一个连续的且具有连续一阶导数的一元函数 $y = f(x) $，它满足条件$ f(x_0) = y_0$ 同时：$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = -\frac{F_{x}}{F_y}$$通过对该式子进行积分可得函数$ y=f(x) $:$$ y = \int{\frac{dy}{dx} dx} =-\int{\frac{F_{x}}{F_y}}dx$$

## 隐函数存在定理2: 
设函数$ F(x, y, z) $在点$ P(x_0, y_0, z_0)$的某个邻域内具有连续的偏导数，且满足$ F(x_, y_0, z_0) = 0$且，$ F_z(x_0, y_0,z_0) \neq 0$ 那么方程$ F(x, y,z) = 0 $在点$ P(x_0, y_0, z_0)$的某个邻域内恒能唯一确定一个连续且具有连续偏导数的多元函数$ z = f(x, y, z)$ 该函数满足$ z_0 = f(x_0, y_0, z_0)$ 同时：$$ \frac{\partial{z}}{\partial{x}} =  -\frac{F_{x}}{F_{z}}  \\ \space \\ \frac{\partial{z}}{\partial{y}} = - \frac{F_{y}}{F_z}$$
 

 # About Double Formula
## 隐函数存在定理3 ：
设函数$ F(x, y ,u, v)$ ,$ G(x, y,u,v)$在点$ P(x_0, y_0, u_0, v_0)$处的某个邻域内具有对各个变量连续的一阶偏导数，同时满足：$$ F(x_0, y_0, u_0, v_0) = 0 \\ G(x_0, y_0, u_0, v_0) = 0 $$同时，偏导数所组成的行列式（即Jacobian矩阵）在点$P(x_0, y_0, u_0, v_0)$处不为0，即：
$$ J =
\left|
\begin{array}{ccc}
\frac{\partial{F}}{\partial{u}} & \frac{\partial{F}}{\partial{v}} \\
\frac{\partial{G}}{\partial{u}} & \frac{\partial{G}}{\partial{v}} \\
\end{array}
\right| \neq 0
$$

那么，方程组$$ F(x, y,u,v) = 0  \\ G(x, y,u,v) = 0$$在点$ P $的某个邻域内唯一确定一组方程：$$ u = u(x, y) \\ v = v(x, y) $$且满足：$$ u_0 = u(x_0, y_0) \\ v_0 = v(x_0, y_0)$$ 并有：$$ \frac{\partial{u}}{\partial{x}} = -\frac{1}{J} \frac{\partial{(F, G)}}{\partial{(x, v)}} \\ \space \\  \frac{\partial{v}}{\partial{x}} = -\frac{1}{J} \frac{\partial{(F, G)}}{\partial{(u, x)}} \\ \space  \\ \frac{\partial{u}}{\partial{y}} = -\frac{1}{J} \frac{\partial{(F, G)}}{\partial{(y, v)}} \\ \space \\ \frac{\partial{u}}{\partial{x}} = -\frac{1}{J} \frac{\partial{(F, G)}}{\partial{(u, y)}}$$
